How Read Along Works

This tool listens through your microphone as you read the Book of Mormon aloud. Words highlight in real time to help you track your place.

1Tap a book, then choose a chapter
2Tap Start Reading — allow microphone access when prompted
3Read aloud at your own pace. Words highlight as you go.

Works best in Chrome on desktop or Android. Safari has limited speech recognition support.
